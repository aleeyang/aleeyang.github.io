Overview

I am a PhD student at Fudan University. During my master’s studies, I was fortunate to be mentored by Hwee Kuan, who not only guided my academic growth but also shaped my perspectives and aspirations.

Previously, I obtained my M.Eng. from Beijing Normal University and my B.Eng. from Xihua University.

Research Interests

My research focuses on the creation, editing, and understanding of videos and visual media, aiming to build human–AI collaborative intelligence that enhances creativity and productivity.
