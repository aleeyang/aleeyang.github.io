About Me

I am currently a PhD student at Fudan University. During my master's studies, I was fortunate to be mentored by Hwee Kuan Lee, who guided my academic growth and shaped my perspectives and aspirations.

Previously, I obtained my M.Eng. from Beijing Normal University and my B.Eng. from Xihua University.

My research interests lie in computer vision and generative AI, with a focus on the creation, editing, and understanding of videos and visual media for human-AI collaborative intelligence.
